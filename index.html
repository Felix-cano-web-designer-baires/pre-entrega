Who is Cristopher Nolan?

Christopher Nolan was born in London in 1970. A prodigious filmmaking talent from an early age, he has built a
brilliant a career on atmospheric films and is credited with breathing new life into the genre of intelligent
action and war films, most particularly the Batman trilogy and Dunkirk.
